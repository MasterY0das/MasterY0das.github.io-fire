Welcome

Wildfires are one of the most significant natural threats facing California. Our student-led project aims to raise awareness, provide insights into wildfire causes and impacts, and encourage preventive measures that can save lives and property.

Why It Matters

Every year, California faces devastating wildfires that destroy homes, wildlife habitats, and lives. With climate change, these fires are becoming more frequent and severe. Education and community action are key to making a difference.
